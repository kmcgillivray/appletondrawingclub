You're invited to join us at for a fun and _free_ casual drawing club hangout where we draw each other's portraits! Everyone will have a chance to take turns posing for short 10 minute portraits in small groups while drawing and hanging out with their friends, neighbors, and fellow artists. Everyone is welcome–all ages, all experience levels, regulars and new artists.

## Celebrate two years of Appleton Drawing Club!

This is also our Appleton Drawing Club two year anniversary party!

- We'll have festive snacks and drinks, or bring your own
- Bring your favorite drawings and paintings from the past year to share in an impromptu gallery (open to anything you've made!).

## Agenda

- Pre-session: Meet at Photo Opp and settle in! Portraits start at 7:00 but feel free to arrive early for socializing and good cheer
- Join a small group of 4-5 artists to take turns posing for 10 minute portraits
- We'll take a short break half way through and then find a new group of artists
- Hang out after the portraits for more social time and merriment!
