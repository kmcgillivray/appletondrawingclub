Explore the history of portraiture in this themed long pose portrait session. Our model will pose in costume inspired by historical royal portraits, brought to life for the contemporary world. Enjoy two hours of stretching your portrait drawing skills with great lighting, an inspiring live model, and a friendly community.

Questions? Check out our [frequently asked questions](/faq)!

## Agenda

- Pre-session: find a spot and set up your materials!
- Welcome and introduction
- Two hour pose, short breaks every 30-40 minutes
- Clean up and share your work on the drawing pile!
