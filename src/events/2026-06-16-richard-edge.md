Join us for a special **live music and drawing session** with our guest and model, [Richard Edge](https://www.instagram.com/richard__edge/)! Richard is on tour for their debut album release, _Vespers_, produced by local Appleton-based record label, [Crutch of Memory](https://www.instagram.com/crutchofmemory/). Richard will bring their summer tour to the drawing club, a unique chance to combine art and music in one great evening. Sketch Richard during the set, and also during a short posed portrait half way through the session.

## Did you say you "can't draw"??

Well, if you'd like to enjoy the music and see the art without drawing yourself, you are more than welcome to join us and hang out! We'll also have extra drawing materials available in case you get inspired to join in for drawing after all. Our sessions are for everyone from complete beginners to pro artists, so no matter what your experience level we hope you'll share a creative night with us.

## About Vespers

From Crutch of Memory:

> Richard Edge's debut LP, Vespers, plays like a stoned Southern ode to the primitive guitar.
>
> A rushing flood of steel-stringed vibration; pristine; plummeting: higher music not made for a concert hall. Casual like the rubber burning off a pair of shitty sneakers on a cooked, overgrown summer sidewalk. It's Richard's vernacular: deeper than dumb- in fact, incredibly deep- a universe of buoyantly drifting particles settling in a creek bed after some buck-toothed kid in cutoff jeans made that poor-man's ocean skin their landing pad.
>
> Call it folk; call it blues; call it punk. It’s an outsider classical music with inherent twang. It's a window into Richard's world, and it's the latest installment in [Crutch of Memory](https://www.crutchofmemory.com)'s irreverent catalog.
