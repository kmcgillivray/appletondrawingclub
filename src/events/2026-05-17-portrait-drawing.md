Welcome to portrait drawing with Appleton Drawing Club! Enjoy two hours of drawing or painting with our wonderful model, Sophia. This is an open drawing session for all experience levels and mediums. Sophia will hold one pose for the session with a few short breaks, so it's the perfect chance to stretch your portrait skills with a live model.

Questions? Check out our [frequently asked questions](/faq)!

## Agenda

- Pre-session: find a spot and set up your materials!
- Welcome and introduction
- Two hour pose, short breaks every 30-40 minutes
- Clean up and share your work on the drawing pile!
